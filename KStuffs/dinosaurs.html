DESCRIPTION

Wooden toys are playthings primerily made from wood, often with additional components from other materials like paint or metal. They are known for their durability, safety, and ability to stimulate imaginative and open-ended play. Wooden toys are considered environmental friendly and sustainable, as wood is renewable resourse

Key Features and Benefits:

  • Durability:
    Wooden toys are generally more durable than plastic toys and can withstand rough play, often lasting for generations.
  • Safety:
    Wood is a natural material, and wooden toys are less likely to break into small, dangerous pieces.
  • Open-ended Play:
    Wooden toys often encourage children to use their imagination and creativity, allowing them to explore different ways to play.
  • Tactile Experience:
    The natural feel of wood provides a sensory experience that can help children develope their sense of touch
