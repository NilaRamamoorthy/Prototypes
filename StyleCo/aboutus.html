Mission
Our mission is to improve the performance and lives of sports and physical fitness participants
in Maldivies. We do that by presenting world leading brands,integrated with exceptional service, unparalleled support, and operational excellence tofacilitate sports and play.

Vision
We envision a future where Maldives embraces healthy living as part of our culture
Bigger Better Brands

Health
Sonee Sports will invest in promoting healthy lifestyles through play and sport because healthy
people contribute to improving the society by channelling the physical and mental energy
